About
Namaskar Parents,

B's Learning Centre is a wholistic approach to introduce Hindi language to young kids. My name is Bhawna Chhatwal, and I am an experienced Hindi Teacher. I have over 10 years of experience teaching Hindi. I have taught students of various levels, including young kids, high school teenagers, and adults. I grew up in the vibrant city of New Delhi where I did my earlier education and graduated from Delhi University with a business major. Having lived in Delhi, Shanghai, Vietnam, Michigan and now Austin with my husband and daughter, I have realized the need to educate young kids with the amazing Hindi language. I started with a small batch with my daughter and few friends in Shanghai to introduce them to Hindi. Through my course work, I introduced Indian culture and mannerism to my daughter and her friends in a foreign land, and I am blessed to keep the tradition going in Michigan and Austin. My coursework is very adaptive and personalized. I prepare lessons keeping in mind each students’ needs and learning pace. In my classes, you will find-
  • Simple and clear explanations
  • Engaging and Interactive exercises
  • Introduction to Indian culture and festivals
  • Special focus on grammar and “Varnamala”
  • Focus course work for high school kids testing out Hindi language for extra credits.
I am proud to say that I have got overwhelming love and affection from my student and parent community in USA and happy to say my kids are recieving high school credits as well.
